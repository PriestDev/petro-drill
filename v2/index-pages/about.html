About Us

Learn more about Petro-Drill Global's mission, vision, and expertise

Petro-Drill Global is a multinational energy support company with a global approach to multidimensional solutions in oil and gas upstream, mid-stream and downstream integrated project management and service delivery.

Our Mission

To be an evolutionary force to foster innovative technologies to achieve world-class performance for our customer satisfaction. As a company and as individuals, we take great pride in adding to the development of our country.

Our Vision

We place the highest priority on the health and safety of our environment. We strive to earn the confidence and admiration of all our stakeholders and be a people of outstanding capabilities and commitment through our excellent operational system.

About Our Services

Petro-Drill is a multinational Energy servicing company with a global approach to world leading state of the art oil and gas exploration services using our High tech Hydroscan, Orescan and Enhanced Oil Recovery service the EPM technology to detect, confirm, acquire and interpret petrophysical and geological datum of oil and gas reservoirs. We further support our E&P clients to treat their wells which have decline production, EPM is applied to boost a remarkable Oil recovery that sustains steady and optimal production for a long period of the Well lifespan.

Training Programs
