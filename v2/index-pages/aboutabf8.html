Brief

Petro-Drill is a multinational Energy servicing company with a global approach to world leading state of the art oil and gas exploration services using our High tech Hydroscan a, Orescan and Enhanced Oil Recovery service the EPM technology to detect, confirm, acquire and interpret petrophysical and geological datum of oil and gas reservoirs. We further support our E&P clients to treat their wells which have decline production, EPM is applied to boost a remarkable Oil recovery that sustains steady and optimal production for a long period of the Well lifespan.
