Our Services

Sourcing Services for Geophysical Data Acquisition and Enhanced Oil Recovery

Petro-Drill undertakes activities relevant to Geophysical services to assist Oil and Gas companies’ operational performance and profitability. Whether your need is to conduct geophysical data acquisition and analysis or identification of Hydrocarbon accumulation or servicing non productive wells using the EPM as our method of Enhanced Oil Recovery application. Petro-Drill has the latest technology, partners and infrastructure to handle any stream of the project from turnkey solution to component consulting support. We can plan, design, build, test, analyze and negotiate your geophysical service expectations as well as train your staff adequately.

 

Sourcing Service for Equipment and Capital Spare Parts

Sourcing of Oil field Rigs, Equipment and spare parts can be a time consuming job. Let Petro-Drill Global do this job for you! We will normally provide a quotation according to your specification within a few days, or we will inform you that we are not able to quote. If you have an urgent requirement, please state this in your request. There is no cost on your hand until you decide to buy. Petro-Drill Global can also organize third party inspections, refurbishment and transport of Rigs and Oilfield equipment to your location by road, air or sea.
