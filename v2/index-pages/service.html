Oil Field Development and Enhance Oil Recovery Services

Our Exploration and Production Unit works in partnership with our EPM partners the Geo-exploration Servies in Germany to help our numerous Oil Operators with an enhanced Oil Recovery service on their producing wells for a better production and Oil recovery. Electrokinetic Pore-fluid Mixer – EPM– is an innovative enhanced oil recovery (EOR) technology, which is based on electrical stimulation of paired production wells. Usually 60-80% of oil volume is captured in fine pores which is not recoverable by water flooding and pumping. An EPM can mobilize a large amount of this additional oil by using existing infrastructure without need of an additional injection wells. It is an eco friendly and very low cost method in comparison to conventional EOR measures like chemical flooding, gas injection or thermal recovery

Petro-Drill Global partners with GET on her high-tech Airborne Electromagnetic Subsurface Hydrocarbon data mapping and processing. Our clients are provided with a complimentary but stand alone High capacity subsurface reservoir data survey in 3-D and process data acquired within record time for real time Drilling plan and design. This service is applicable to areas and locations where the conventional Seismic service has limitations, the Hydroscan has been tested in several fields and has an amazing excellent results of detection of the availability of oil and gas in a field. It has saved several Operators millions of the USD by pre-determining the availability or absence of Hydrocarbon in any leased area and had caused our Clients to make an informed real time decision in investment. This service is very affordable and hs a very short turn around time from start of survey maps to data acquisition and processing.

Electrokinetic Pore-fluid Mixer (EPM) – Innovative Oil Recovery Enhancement 

Electrokinetic Pore-fluid Mixer – EPM – is a new oil recovery enhancement (EOR) technology, which is based on galvanic injection of controlled current sweeps  into the pay while production. It is a highly efficient EOR system because of low operating costs, non-invasive, ecofriendly and very low investment in comparison to conventional EOR measures of chemical flooding, gas injection or thermal recovery.

Due to cost factors every well can be connected and stranded oil reservoirs can be resolved
